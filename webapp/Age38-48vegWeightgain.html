Vegetarian Daily Meal Plan

Morning (7:00 AM – 9:00 AM)

  • Warm water with flax seeds
  • Multigrain toast with avocado spread
  • 1 glass of fresh pomegranate juice

Mid-Morning (11:00 AM)

  • Fresh coconut water
  • Mixed fruit bowl (grapes, papaya, pear)

Lunch (1:00 PM – 2:00 PM)

  • Quinoa salad with beans and chickpeas
  • Steamed broccoli and carrots
  • 1 cup moong dal with turmeric
  • Mint buttermilk

Evening Snack (5:00 PM)

  • Roasted fox nuts (makhana) with spices
  • 1 cup green tea

Dinner (7:30 PM – 9:00 PM)

  • Vegetable stew with tofu
  • 2 small millet rotis
  • Chamomile tea before bed
