Non-Vegetarian Daily Meal Plan

Morning (7:00 AM – 9:00 AM)

  • Warm water with ginger and honey
  • Boiled eggs with multigrain toast
  • 1 glass of low-fat milk or black coffee

Mid-Morning (11:00 AM)

  • Grilled chicken strips or turkey slices
  • 1 seasonal fruit (like guava or apple)

Lunch (1:00 PM – 2:00 PM)

  • Brown rice or chapati
  • Grilled fish or chicken curry (less oil)
  • Steamed vegetables and green salad
  • Curd or buttermilk

Evening Snack (5:00 PM)

  • Boiled egg sandwich or chicken soup
  • 1 cup green tea

Dinner (7:30 PM – 9:00 PM)

  • Grilled or baked salmon/chicken breast
  • Stir-fried vegetables
  • Warm turmeric milk or chamomile tea before bed
