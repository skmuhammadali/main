Vegetarian Daily Meal Plan

Morning (6:30 AM – 8:30 AM)

  • Warm turmeric water with chia seeds
  • Vegetable upma with coconut chutney
  • 1 cup almond milk or soy milk

Mid-Morning (10:30 AM)

  • Seasonal fruits (mango, pomegranate, grapes)
  • 1 cup green tea

Lunch (12:30 PM – 2:00 PM)

  • Roti made with mixed grains or paratha with ghee
  • Bhindi masala or bottle gourd curry
  • Chana dal with steamed rice
  • Cucumber-tomato salad

Evening Snack (4:30 PM – 5:30 PM)

  • Roasted makhana (fox nuts) with herbs
  • Masala buttermilk or mint lemonade

Dinner (7:00 PM – 8:30 PM)

  • Vegetable soup with whole wheat toast
  • Grilled paneer cubes or tofu stir fry
  • Chamomile tea before bed
