Vegetarian Weight Loss Meal Plan

Morning (7:00 AM – 9:00 AM)

  • Warm lemon water with chia seeds
  • 1 bowl vegetable poha with sprouts
  • Green tea

Mid-Morning (10:30 AM)

  • 1 apple or pear
  • Handful of pumpkin seeds

Lunch (1:00 PM)

  • Brown rice with dal and sautéed greens
  • Mixed raw vegetable salad with lemon
  • Low-fat curd

Evening (4:30 PM)

  • Herbal tea or black coffee
  • Roasted soy nuts or makhana

Dinner (7:30 PM)

  • Clear vegetable soup
  • Grilled tofu or paneer
  • Stir-fried zucchini and bell peppers
