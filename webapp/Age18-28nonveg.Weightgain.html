Non-Vegetarian Daily Meal Plan

Morning (7:00 AM – 9:00 AM)

  • Warm lemon water + 4 soaked almonds
  • Boiled eggs (2) with brown bread toast
  • 1 glass of low-fat milk

Mid-Morning (11:00 AM)

  • Fruit salad (mango, watermelon, kiwi)
  • Green tea or buttermilk

Lunch (1:00 PM – 2:00 PM)

  • Grilled chicken breast or fish curry
  • Brown rice or chapati (2)
  • Steamed veggies + green salad

Evening Snack (5:00 PM)

  • Boiled egg salad or chicken soup
  • 1 glass of coconut water

Dinner (7:30 PM – 9:00 PM)

  • Grilled fish or chicken stir fry with veggies
  • 1 multigrain roti or small portion of rice
  • Low-fat curd or soup before bed
