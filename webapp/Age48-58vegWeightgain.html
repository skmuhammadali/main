Vegetarian Daily Meal Plan

Morning (7:00 AM – 9:00 AM)

  • Lukewarm water with chia seeds
  • Vegetable upma with coconut chutney
  • 1 glass of fresh watermelon juice

Mid-Morning (11:00 AM)

  • Buttermilk with roasted cumin
  • 1 small bowl of boiled corn and cucumber

Lunch (1:00 PM – 2:00 PM)

  • 2 multigrain rotis or 1 bowl millet rice
  • Palak dal or bottle gourd curry
  • Carrot-beetroot salad with lemon
  • 1 small bowl curd

Evening Snack (5:00 PM)

  • Roasted lotus seeds (makhana)
  • Mint green tea

Dinner (7:30 PM – 9:00 PM)

  • Vegetable stew with red rice appam
  • 1 bowl mixed dal soup
  • Warm cinnamon water before bed
