Non-Vegetarian Weight Loss Meal Plan

Morning (7:00 AM – 9:00 AM)

  • Warm water with lemon and chia seeds
  • Boiled eggs (2 whites, 1 whole)
  • 1 slice multigrain toast
  • Black coffee (no sugar)

Mid-Morning (10:30 AM)

  • 1 apple or guava
  • Green tea

Lunch (1:00 PM)

  • Grilled chicken breast (100g)
  • Steamed vegetables (carrot, beans, broccoli)
  • 1 small bowl brown rice

Evening (4:30 PM)

  • Buttermilk or green tea
  • Handful of almonds or walnuts

Dinner (7:30 PM)

  • Fish curry or grilled fish (lightly spiced)
  • Mixed vegetable soup
  • Cucumber and tomato salad
